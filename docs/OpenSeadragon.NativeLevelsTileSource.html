Class: NativeLevelsTileSource

NativeLevelsTileSource(levels)

The NativeLevelsTileSource allows specific levels of image pyramids to be loaded into an OpenSeadragon Viewer instead of assuming that all powers of two are ok. This allows image servers to avoid server-side conversion of images to non-native resolutions.
